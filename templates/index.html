BUBT 5th Convocation

5th January 2022

Bangabandhu International Conference Center

From Vice Chancellor

Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT) is one of the top-ranking universities in Bangladesh which started its journey in the year 2003. I am happy to present to you our university and welcome you to be involved in the progress of the university as its stakeholders. Over the last 17 years, BUBT has grown its stature and is now regarded as a globally recognized university. It is now members of prestigious organizations like Association of Commonwealth Universities, the Association of International Universities and the Association of Management Development institutions in South Asia. At present BUBT offers 20 different undergraduate and post graduate programs under five faculties namely: i) Faculty of Business ii) Faculty of Engineering and Applied Sciences iii) Faculty of Arts & Humanities iv) Faculty of Social Sciences and v) Faculty of Law. Very soon, BUBT is going to start B.Sc in Civil Engineering with the approval from the University Grants Commission (UGC).
